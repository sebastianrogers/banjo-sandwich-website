About the Band

Banjo Sandwich is a dedicated bluegrass ensemble committed to preserving and celebrating the timeless traditions of American folk music. Our sound combines classic bluegrass instrumentation with heartfelt vocals and tight harmonies that transport audiences to the heart of Appalachian musical heritage.

Whether performing at intimate venues or large festivals, we bring energy, authenticity, and a deep love for the music that has shaped generations of American culture.
