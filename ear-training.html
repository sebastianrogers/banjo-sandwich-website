Ear Training

Develop your musical ear for banjo and bluegrass

Why Ear Training?

Learn by Listening

Ear training helps you learn songs faster by recognizing patterns and intervals in bluegrass music.

Improve Your Playing

Better ear training leads to better timing, pitch accuracy, and the ability to play with others.

Play What You Hear

Develop the ability to play songs you hear without sheet music or tabs.

Exercises and Tips

Interval Recognition

Practice identifying the distance between notes. Start with common bluegrass intervals like thirds and fifths.

Rhythm Training

Tap along to bluegrass songs and try to replicate the rhythmic patterns you hear.

Active Listening

Listen to recordings and try to pick out individual banjo parts, licks, and techniques.
